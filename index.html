About

This project showcases a Over-The-Top (OTT) platform designed exclusively using HTML and CSS, focusing on a sleek and user-friendly front-end interface. The platform simulates the structure and appearance of modern streaming services, featuring sections for featured content, categorized movie or series thumbnails, and a responsive layout for seamless viewing across devices.
